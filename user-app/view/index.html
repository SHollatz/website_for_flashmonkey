The advanced learning tool the way you need it

Simple, Flexible, Genius

FlashMonkey lets you create flashcards that fit any specific information you are interested in. You can use all kinde of media, including video, audio, screenshots, graphs, etc. Use your cards for memorizing or for testing your knowledge. FlashMonkey generates an automatic test based off these cards that adjust as you progress making correct or incorrect choices.
